Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate is this unit converter?

Our unit converter provides highly accurate results with up to 8 decimal places. All conversions are based on standard international conversion factors.

Which units can I convert?

You can convert various units including length (meters, feet), weight (kilograms, pounds), volume (liters, gallons), and temperature (Celsius, Fahrenheit, Kelvin).

Is this converter free to use?

Yes, our unit converter is completely free to use. No registration or signup is required.

Can I use this converter offline?

Currently, our converter requires an internet connection. However, most modern browsers will cache the page, allowing it to work with limited functionality even when offline.

Are the conversion rates up to date?

Yes, we use standard international conversion rates that are constant for physical units like length, weight, and volume. For temperature, the conversions are based on mathematical formulas that never change.
